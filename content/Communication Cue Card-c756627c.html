Communication Cue Card

A cue card is a tool that can remind you of the behaviors that are most important to showing each competency. Each behavior listed on the card will help “cue” your behavior in order to practice and show that behavior while you are at work. As you work through the content and practice at work, it is helpful to keep your cue card with you to remind yourself of the behaviors needed to show your competence in this area. You can print the cue card from this page or use the QuILTSS cue cards on a ring that you received for the program.
